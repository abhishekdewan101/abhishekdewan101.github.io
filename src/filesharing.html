Contextual File Sharing

Summary

Aim
The idea of contextually aware file sharing was aimed to follow the sharing principles of snapchat, whereby users can temporarily share files with other users as long all of them stay in the same proximity.

Solution
Using low powered bluetooth beacons and a backend server, the system is able to keep track of all people within the proximity of a given bluetooth beacon and then allow the sharing of files between them.

Role
Android Developer | Desing Technologist

Project Dates
June 2014 - August 2014

Prototype

The prototype is a mixture of two seperate elements. A sharing intent that brings up the following screens in order to allow for a simple single click sharing action to share to either devices that are near you or to people you know or to your favourite apps. The other part of the app is the gallery that allows users to see the document that has been shared with them and the files they have shared. With a single click the user is able to revoke access from anyone.
