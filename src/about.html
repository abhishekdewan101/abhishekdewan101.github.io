About me

Product Designer || Creative Hacker

Hi! My name is Abhishek and I'd like to thank you for taking the time to visit my website. During the day, I work as a product designer for Samsung Research America, trying to come up with the next generation user experience for Samsung's flagship products. Utilizing my design and technology background, I work closely with my design team to help solve problems with our interaction models, user research analysis and even create high fidelity prototypes for user testing scenarios.

To help me take my mind of work, I turn to my other passions in life, cooking and gaming. I have been cooking for almost 7 years now and I find that it helps me destress. Similarly, gaming has been a big part of my life and an inspiration to learn programming in the first place.

If you'd like to get in touch with me either about my work or my food, please feel free to send me an email from the form below.
